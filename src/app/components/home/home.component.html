EURO KONKRETE BUILDING DYNAMICS LLC.

Euro Konkrete prides itself on the diverse portfolio of waterproofing solutions, providing its customers with the most competitive price while maintaining high quality and reliability that they all come to expect

Euro Konkrete Building Dynamics LLC., is an offshoot of Pudlo Middle East Building Materials LLC., (PME) which was part of the DB (Holdings) Group Limited, UK, after PME decided to shut down their middle east operations effective 30th June 2020, unable to sustain the losses owing to the pandemic and envisaging further losses in the coming months. The entire present team at Euro Konkrete was part of PME right from Regional Director / General Manager Mr. Harold Solomon to all other staff and have retained the same office, warehouse and communication lines as that of Pudlo Middle East. (www.pudlo.ae)

Euro Konkrete are now the authorized promoters of waterproof concrete admixture PUDLO CWP and with this newly floated business entity is poised to take forward the business to retain and capitalize on this well established brand in the UAE present in this part of the region for the past more than 20 years.
